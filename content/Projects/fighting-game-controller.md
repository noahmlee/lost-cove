## Custom Leverless Fight Stick

This was a personal project that I started In the weeks leading up to the release of Street Fighter 6. It's a custom USB-C leverless controller specifically designed for fighting games. 

## What is a fight stick?

A fight stick (or arcade stick) is a gaming controller that is in the style of a classic arcade cabinet interface. Traditionally these include some amount of arcade style buttons, and most noticeably a lever or "stick". There are other shapes and configurations that fight sticks can come in, such my leverless controller pictured above, but traditionally they will look more like the controller pictured below.

## Why build your own?

While there are a plethora of ready-to-go, pre-made options to choose from online, building your own custom solution can have some pretty appealing pros!

### Pros:
- Its a very fun and rewarding project *(this is the biggest reason)*.
- You can build the exact controller that you are looking for.
- Cheaper than buying a pre-built **SOMETIMES**!

### Cons:
- Wiring can be physically tedious.
- Finding certain parts in-stock can be challenging around the release of popular fighting games.
- Can certainly be more expensive than pre-made options depending on your build.

## My build

Okay time to brag about my build. I decided to go with a leverless controller which unlike traditional fight sticks, had no arcade style joystick. There are only buttons. Buttons for movement, buttons for attacks, buttons for menus, just... buttons. 
I decided on a leverless controller for two main reasons:
1. I already had a controller with a lever.
2. I have not built up the dexterity to effectively use a lever.

After deciding what type of controller I wanted to make it was time to procure parts. I previously mentioned that this idea struck my right before the release of Street Fighter 6, which as of today is still one of the most popular fighting games. Due to this fact, parts were difficult to source, but after a few days of digging and deciding I had found and ordered everything I needed.
